Premier Waterfront Community

A New Standard for Riverside Living

Rivergate Bordentown is a transit-oriented waterfront community by Sterling Properties, offering a luxurious collection of one and two-bedroom residences perched along the Delaware River in historic Bordentown, New Jersey.

Designed for discerning renters who demand both comfort and connectivity, Rivergate places the River Line Light Rail at your doorstep — with direct access to Trenton, and convenient connections to NYC, Philadelphia, and the Jersey Shore.

Community Amenities

Resort-Style Living, Every Day

The Rivergate Clubhouse anchors an elevated lifestyle — fitness studio, resort-style heated pool, outdoor BBQ, professionally landscaped grounds, and secured access throughout.

Every apartment is finished with shaker espresso cabinetry, quartz countertops, stainless appliances, 9-foot ceilings, private balcony, and in-unit washer/dryer.
