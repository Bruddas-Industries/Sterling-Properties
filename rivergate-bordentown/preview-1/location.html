Transit-Oriented Living

The Region at Your Doorstep

Rivergate was intentionally developed as a transit-oriented community. The Bordentown River Line station is steps from your front door — providing direct, car-free access to Trenton's Transit Center and connections to the entire Northeast Corridor.

From Trenton, NJ Transit and Amtrak connect you north to New York Penn Station and south to Philadelphia 30th Street Station. Whether you commute daily or travel occasionally, Rivergate keeps you connected without the car.

  • — Bordentown River Line Station — steps away
  • — Trenton Transit Center — ~10 min · NJ Transit + Amtrak
  • — New York Penn Station — ~75 min by rail
  • — Philadelphia 30th Street — ~45 min by rail
  • — I-295, I-195, Route 206 & NJ Turnpike nearby
