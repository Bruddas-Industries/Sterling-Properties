Community Guidelines

Living at Rivergate

A brief overview of key community policies. Full details are available in your lease agreement and community rules document.

Parking
  • — Surface parking and secured garage available
  • — All vehicles must be registered with the leasing office
  • — Visitor parking available in designated areas
  • — No commercial vehicles or RVs
Noise & Quiet Hours
  • — Quiet hours: 10pm – 8am on weekdays
  • — Quiet hours: 11pm – 9am on weekends
  • — Loud music or disturbances may result in lease violation
  • — Be considerate of shared walls and common areas
Pool & Amenity Hours
  • — Pool open seasonally — Memorial Day through Labor Day
  • — Pool hours posted at the clubhouse
  • — Residents must present key fob for access
  • — No lifeguard on duty — swim at your own risk
Packages & Deliveries
  • — Package delivery instructions provided at move-in
  • — Contact leasing office for package pickup procedures
  • — Large deliveries require advance notice to management
Maintenance
  • — Submit requests through the online portal (AppFolio)
  • — Emergency maintenance available 24/7 by phone
  • — Routine maintenance responded to within 1–3 business days
  • — Residents responsible for reporting issues promptly
Move-Out
  • — 60-day written notice required prior to vacating
  • — Schedule a move-out inspection with the leasing office
  • — Return all keys and fobs on move-out day
  • — Security deposit returned per NJ state law
