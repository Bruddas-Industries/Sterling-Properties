Amenities

Community Hub

The Rivergate Clubhouse

The Rivergate Clubhouse serves as the beating heart of the community — an elegantly appointed gathering space for residents to socialize, relax, and connect. Whether hosting a private event or enjoying a quiet afternoon, the clubhouse sets a tone of refined, resort-style living.

Adjacent to the fitness studio and overlooking the landscaped grounds and heated pool, the clubhouse places every cornerstone amenity within steps of one another.

Outdoor Living

Resort-Style Pool & Grounds

The resort-style heated outdoor swimming pool and sun deck offer a private retreat that rivals any five-star resort. Professionally landscaped and irrigated grounds surround the property, creating a lush, tranquil setting year-round.

A dedicated outdoor BBQ and entertaining area sits just off the pool — perfect for weekend gatherings, summer cookouts, and casual evenings with neighbors.
