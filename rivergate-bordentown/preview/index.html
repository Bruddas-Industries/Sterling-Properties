Premier Waterfront Community

A New Standard for Riverside Living

Rivergate Bordentown is a transit-oriented waterfront community by Sterling Properties, offering a luxurious collection of one and two-bedroom residences perched along the Delaware River in historic Bordentown, New Jersey.

Designed for discerning renters who demand both comfort and connectivity, Rivergate places the River Line Light Rail at your doorstep — with direct access to Trenton, and convenient connections to NYC, Philadelphia, and the Jersey Shore.

Resort-Style Living, Every Day

Location

Connected to Everywhere That Matters

Situated at 500 Bluff View Circle in historic Bordentown, NJ, Rivergate sits at the crossroads of natural beauty and urban convenience. The River Line Light Rail makes daily commutes effortless, while nearby I-295, I-195, Route 206, and the NJ Turnpike keep the entire region within reach.

Transit

  • River Line Light Rail — steps away
  • Trenton Transit Center (NJ Transit / Amtrak)
  • I-295, I-195, Route 206, NJ Turnpike
  • Easy access to NYC, Philadelphia & Princeton

Shopping

  • Bordentown City boutiques & dining
  • Hamilton Marketplace
  • Shoppes at Hamilton
  • Quaker Bridge Mall

Recreation

  • Old York Country Club & Rancocas Golf
  • Crystal Lake Park & Kuser Farm Park
  • Grounds For Sculpture
  • The Jersey Shore
